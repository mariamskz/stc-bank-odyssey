Title Card — Campaign Brief

In Kubrick's masterpiece, early man discovered a monolith — a single object that reorganized all intelligence, all behavior, all possibility.

For a decade, STC was Saudi Arabia's everyday tap. Fast. Useful. Present. But STC Bank is not a faster tap. It is the monolith.

Not Back to the Future — no one is going back. This is 2001: The financial architecture Saudi Arabia was always moving toward. Lucid. Anticipatory. Sovereign. Seamless. Grounded.

The campaign introduces a single human anchor: Rayyanah Barnawi — the first Saudi woman in space, the first Arab woman to orbit Earth, and the 600th person in the history of humanity to leave it. A scientist, not a pilot. A threshold-crosser, not a passenger. She is the bridge between Saudi human trust and digital infinite.

Six Products — Six Scenes

its own chapter
of the odyssey.

Scene I

Credit Cards

بطاقات الائتمان

"Your spending, engineered to work for you."

Not a limit — a command. The card as mission instrument. Cashback that arrives structured, not as a surprise. Rewards that compound like a well-designed system. The language: sovereign, never promotional. The visual: the card itself is monolith black.

Sovereign Lucid

Scene II

Personal Finance

التمويل الشخصي

"Your future salary. Present now."

Not a loan — an advance against the future you're already building. The campaign visual: a clock face replaced by a financial chart. Rayyanah Barnawi voiceover: "I planned for an eight-day mission. STC Bank helped me plan for an eight-year future." No rate-first messaging. Trust-first, structure-second.

Anticipatory Grounded

Scene III

Remittance

التحويلات المالية

"Send money. Send home."

Kubrick showed us that distance is an illusion of perspective. From space, Earth looks singular. From STC Bank, sending money home feels the same — instant, invisible, already done. The campaign shows the recipient's face. Not the sender's phone screen. Human trust, delivered digitally.

Seamless Grounded

Scene IV

Marketplace

المتجر

"Everything your financial life needs. One orbit."

The space station in 2001 is a hub — multiple missions departing from one intelligent center. The Marketplace is STC Bank's station. Insurance, offers, financial tools, curated by intelligence. Language: structured and curatorial. Never bazaar-like. Never loud.

Lucid Anticipatory

Scene V

eSIM

الشريحة الرقمية

"Same network. Any sky."

Rayyanah Barnawi connected from the ISS. You connect from anywhere. The eSIM campaign uses the astronaut image at its most literal — but the product copy remains understated. No technical specs. Just the image of someone connected across the most extreme distance, and the quiet confidence that yours will work just as well.

Seamless Sovereign

Scene VI

Savings Account

حساب الادخار

"Tomorrow, organized. Today."

HAL tracks the mission. STC Bank tracks your savings. The campaign visualizes savings goals as mission milestones — a progress bar that looks like a docking approach. The Arabic line: "اليوم تدّخر. غداً تملك." Today you save. Tomorrow you own. Aspirational without hype. Structured without coldness.
